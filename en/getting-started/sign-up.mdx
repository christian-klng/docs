You can sign up for Revisory in three ways: via email, with your Google account, or with GitHub.

## Sign Up with Email

1. Go to [app.revisory.ai](https://app.revisory.ai) and click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your name, email address, and a password
3. Click **Create Account**
4. Check your email inbox and click the confirmation link
5. After confirmation, you can log in

<Tip>
The confirmation link is valid for 24 hours. Check your spam folder if the email doesn't arrive.
</Tip>

## Sign In with Google

1. On the login page, click **Continue with Google**
2. Select your Google account or sign in
3. Allow Revisory to access your name and email address
4. You'll be logged in automatically

## Sign In with GitHub

1. On the login page, click **Continue with GitHub**
2. Sign in to GitHub if needed
3. Authorize the Revisory app
4. You'll be logged in automatically

## After Registration

On your first login, you'll be asked to create an organization or join an existing one:

- **Create Organization:** Choose a name and a unique URL (slug)
- **Accept Invitation:** If you received an invitation link, you'll be redirected to the corresponding organization automatically

<Info>
You can join additional organizations or create new ones later. Your account is not limited to a single organization.
</Info>
