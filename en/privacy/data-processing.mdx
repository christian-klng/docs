Transparency is important to us. Here's what data Revisory processes.

## What data is stored?

### Account data

- Name and email address
- Profile picture (optional)
- Connected login methods (Google, GitHub)

### Organization data

- Organization name and logo
- Team members and their roles
- Billing information

### Content data

- Uploaded documents
- Extracted text from documents
- Categories and evaluation steps
- Analyses and results

## Where is data stored?

All data is stored in the European Union. Revisory uses certified data centers with:

- ISO 27001 certification
- Encryption at rest
- Regular backups

## How long is data stored?

| Data type | Retention |
|-----------|-----------|
| Account data | Until account deletion |
| Documents | Until manual deletion |
| Analyses | Until manual deletion |
| Billing data | 10 years (legal requirement) |
