Your profile contains personal information visible in the app and to team members.

## Opening your profile

Click your avatar in the top right corner and select **Profile**.

## Changing your name

1. Enter your new name in the **Name** field
2. Click **Save**

Your name appears in comments, activity logs, and to other team members.

## Changing your avatar

1. Click your current avatar or the placeholder image
2. Select an image file (PNG, JPG, or GIF)
3. The avatar is automatically cropped and saved

<Tip>
Square images work best. Recommended minimum size: 200Ã—200 pixels.
</Tip>

## Email address

Your email address is used for login and notifications.

<Info>
The email address cannot currently be changed. Contact support if you need to use a new email.
</Info>

## Deleting your account

If you want to delete your account:

1. Scroll to the bottom of the profile page
2. Click **Delete Account**
3. Confirm with your password
4. Your account is permanently deleted

<Warning>
Before deleting, you must leave all organizations where you are the only Owner. Transfer ownership or delete the organization first.
</Warning>
