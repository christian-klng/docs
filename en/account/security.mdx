Protect your account with a secure password and manage your login methods.

## Opening security settings

Click your avatar in the top right corner and select **Security**.

## Changing your password

1. Enter your current password
2. Enter your new password
3. Repeat the new password
4. Click **Change Password**

<Tip>
Use a password with at least 12 characters, including letters, numbers, and special characters.
</Tip>

## Forgot password

If you've forgotten your password:

1. Go to the login page
2. Click **Forgot password?**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your inbox and click the reset link
5. Choose a new password

The reset link is valid for 1 hour.

## Connected accounts

If you signed up with Google or GitHub, you'll see connected accounts here.

### Connecting an account

You can add additional login methods:

1. Click **Connect Google** or **Connect GitHub**
2. Sign in to the respective service
3. Allow the connection

After connecting, you can sign in with any connected method.

### Disconnecting an account

1. Click **Disconnect** next to the connected account
2. Confirm the action

<Warning>
You can only disconnect an account if you have at least one other login method (password or another connected account).
</Warning>

## Active sessions

Under **Active Sessions**, you can see where you're currently logged in:

- Device and browser
- Location (based on IP)
- Last activity

You can end individual sessions or sign out all other sessions at once.
