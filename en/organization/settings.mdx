Personalize your organization with custom branding.

## Opening settings

1. Click **Organization** in the sidebar
2. Select the **Settings** tab

## Changing the name

The organization name appears in navigation and invitations.

1. Enter the new name in the **Name** field
2. Click **Save**

## Changing the URL (slug)

Your organization's URL determines the path in the address bar (e.g., `app.revisory.ai/org/your-slug`).

1. Enter a new slug
2. The slug must be unique and URL-compatible (lowercase letters, hyphens, no spaces)
3. Click **Save**

<Warning>
Changing the slug invalidates existing links. Share the new link with your team.
</Warning>

## Uploading a logo

Your logo appears in the top left corner of the app.

1. Click **Change Logo**
2. Select an image file (PNG, JPG, or SVG)
3. Recommended size: 200Ã—50 pixels
4. The logo is saved automatically

## Primary color

The primary color is used for accents throughout the interface.

1. Click the color field next to **Primary Color**
2. Choose a color in the picker or enter a hex code
3. Click **Save**

<Tip>
Choose a color with good contrast against white, as it often appears on light backgrounds.
</Tip>

## Deleting the organization

<Warning>
Deleting an organization permanently removes all documents, categories, analyses, and team members.
</Warning>

Only the Owner can delete the organization:

1. Scroll to the bottom of settings
2. Click **Delete Organization**
3. Enter the organization name to confirm
4. Click **Delete Permanently**
