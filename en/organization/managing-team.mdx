Revisory supports teamwork: invite colleagues and collaborate on documents and analyses.

## Inviting a member

1. Click **Organization** in the sidebar
2. Select the **Team** tab
3. Click **Invite Member**
4. Enter the email address
5. Choose the role (Member, Admin, or Owner)
6. Click **Send Invitation**

The invited person receives an email with a link to register or join.

<Info>
Invitation links are valid for 7 days. After that, you'll need to send a new invitation.
</Info>

### Member

The default role for team members. Can upload documents, create categories, and run analyses.

### Admin

Extended permissions for team management. Can invite members, change roles, and edit organization settings.

### Owner

Complete control over the organization. Can delete the organization and transfer ownership. Every organization has at least one Owner.

1. Go to **Organization** â†’ **Team**
2. Find the member in the list
3. Click on the current role
4. Select the new role

<Warning>
You cannot downgrade your own role. Another Owner or Admin must do this.
</Warning>

## Removing a member

1. Go to **Organization** â†’ **Team**
2. Click the menu (three dots) next to the member
3. Select **Remove**
4. Confirm the action

Removed members immediately lose access. Their created documents and analyses are preserved.
