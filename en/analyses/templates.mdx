Templates are preconfigured categories with ready-made evaluation steps. They speed up onboarding and ensure consistent standards.

## Using a template

1. Go to **Categories**
2. Click **Create from Template**
3. Browse available templates
4. Select a template
5. Optional: Customize the name
6. Click **Create**

The new category contains all evaluation steps from the template. You can then customize, add, or remove them.

## Available templates

Revisory offers templates for various use cases:

- **Project proposals:** Objectives, resources, timeline, risks
- **Reports:** Structure, completeness, clarity
- **Contracts:** Legal aspects, balance

<Info>
The template library is regularly expanded. Templates are available in multiple languages.
</Info>
