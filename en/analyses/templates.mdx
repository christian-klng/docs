Templates are preconfigured categories with ready-made evaluation steps. They speed up onboarding and ensure consistent standards.

## Using a template

1. Go to **Categories**
2. Click **Create from Template**
3. Browse available templates
4. Select a template
5. Optional: Customize the name
6. Click **Create**

The new category contains all evaluation steps from the template. You can then customize, add, or remove them.

## Available templates

Revisory offers templates for various use cases:

- **Project proposals:** Objectives, resources, timeline, risks
- **Reports:** Structure, completeness, clarity
- **Contracts:** Legal aspects, balance

<Info>
The template library is regularly expanded. Templates are available in multiple languages.
</Info>

## Creating your own template

As an administrator, you can create templates from existing categories:

1. Open a well-configured category
2. Click the menu (three dots)
3. Select **Save as Template**
4. Enter a name and description
5. Choose the language
6. Click **Save**

The template is now available to all members of your organization.

## Templates vs. categories

| Aspect | Template | Category |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Purpose | Blueprint for copying | Active container for analyses |
| Editing | Admins only | Anyone with access |
| Analyses | Not directly possible | Yes |
| Sharing | Organization-wide | Bound to category |
